Draughts

Controls

  • Move your piece by drag-and-drop it with your mouse
  • Double click on a piece to make it a king

Instructions

  • A move consists of moving a piece one square diagonally to an adjacent unoccupied dark square
  • A piece making a non-capturing move (not involving a jump) may move only one square forward
  • Only one piece may be captured in a single jump; however, multiple jumps are allowed on a single turn
  • All capturing moves forward and backward are compulsory
  • When a piece is captured, it is removed from the board (dragged to the grey strip on the right)
  • kings may move and combine jumps multiple squares in any diagonal direction forward and backward
  • A player wins the game when the opponent cannot make a move
  • Have Fun!
